## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are used to remember your preferences, enhance your user experience, and gather analytics data.

## 2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:
-   **Essential Cookies:** These are necessary for the proper functioning of our Services, such as maintaining your session and ensuring security.
-   **Performance Cookies:** These help us understand how you interact with our Services, allowing us to improve performance and usability.
-   **Functionality Cookies:** These remember your preferences and settings to provide a more personalized experience.
-   **Marketing Cookies:** These are used to deliver relevant advertising and track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

## 3. Third-Party Cookies

We may also use cookies from third-party services, such as Google Analytics, to collect and analyze usage data.

## 4. Your Choices

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our Services.

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes, and your continued use of our Services will constitute acceptance of the new policy.
